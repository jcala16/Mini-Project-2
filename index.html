Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

How to make chocolate chip cookies

On this webpage, you will find everything you need to make a delicous batch of chocolate chip cookies

Ingredients

  • 8 tbsp salted butter
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  • Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Melt butter in microwave.
  • Add vanilla, egg, and sugar to butter and beat until creamy
  • Add flour, baking soda, and salt. Form into a dough ball
  • Add chocolate chips and seperate the dough into 12 balls. Place on a cookie sheet
  • Bake for 9-11 minutes, or until golden
  • Enjoy!
